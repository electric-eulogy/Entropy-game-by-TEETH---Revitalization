CONTROLS:
Arrow keys to move
Shift to run
Enter to select
ESC for menu

WALKTHROUGH:
>> AS NONEC <<
- Examine the picture on the wall in the top right corner.
- Go to the "computer" (it looks like an eye) on the wall in the center on the left, and interact with it.
- Interact with the boxes on the screen as follows (Note that you don't have to repeat ones that you have already clicked): Milk, Eyes, Tar, Brain, Sugar, Wire, Flour, Rabbit Foot.

>> AS BREAD <<
- Walk up to the pink rabbit on the stairs and talk to them.
- Collect Painting Five from behind the throne.
- Go to the double doors below the stairs and enter the kitchen.
- Go up to the teapot in the fireplace next to the ladder and interact with it for Painting Six.
- Go to the door at the back of the room and enter the closet.
- Interact with the table by the right wall to get Painting One.
- Go back to the main room of the castle.
- Go up the stairs to the double doors and enter the hallway.
- Enter the double doors to the room where the bunnies are cleaning.
- To navigate the slippery floor to the Royal Spear, do as follows:
	- Go to the right to hit the far crate.
	- Go up to hit the wall.
	- Go right to hit the crumpled rug.
	- Go down to hit the top of the crate you hit before.
	- Go left to hit the second crumpled rug.
	- Go up to hit the table.
	- Go left to hit the crate.
	- Go up and grab the spear.
Now that you have been kicked out of the room (there is no need to navigate out; the game loads you back into the hallway automatically) interact with the dark table with the vanity mirror above it to get Painting Two.
- Go back to the main room and go to the left to leave.
- Go to the gate on the right to get to the maze.
- Go left and into the tent.
- Tell the rabbit that you will help with the mushroom problem. The mushrooms change the color of themselves and/or the ones around them. Don't worry about the mushroom to the right of the rabbit. To solve the puzzle, do as follows:
	- Interact with the second closest mushroom left to the white rabbit.
	- Interact with the second closest mushroom right of the rabbit.
	- Interact with the third closest mushroom to the right of the rabbit.
	- Interact with the mushroom straight across from the rabbit.
	- Interact with the third closest mushroom to the left of the rabbit.

>> AS NONEC <<
- From the starting point in the dream, head straight down and interact with the first door (not the one closest to your right, initially).
- Head to the right and interact with the door that's closest to the right and below you.
- Head all the way to the left, and you'll find some doors shaped in an arrow facing left (<), interact with the middle door.
- For the questions Nonec asks, answer the following (The last question doesn't matter): DISORDER, OUR SINS, A BASTARDS WOE, CONSCIOUSNESS
- Go over to the shower faucet (the big, bendy purple thing) and interact with it. 

>> AS BREAD <<
- Go into the maze and head up, taking the second left.
- Go up and take the right to wrap around to where a bunny is sleeping.
- Interact with the sleeping bunny to get Painting Three.
- Go to the beginning of the maze and take the first right. Follow that road to reach your friend.
- Go back to the castle kitchen (where you found Painting One).
- Go up the ladder to the green-colored pig and interact with it. You will get Painting Four from this interaction.
Select the long skinny knife from the top menu, wave the cursor over the dashed line, and press enter occasionally.
- Select the wire hanger.
- Select the towel and wave the cursor over the areas with the pink arrows; also, press enter around the spots a few times.
- Select the long skinny knife.
- Bring the cursor over the intestines a few times (where the pink arrows are) to clear it, and press enter a few times.
- Select the saw, go down the area with the pink arrows, and press enter as you go.
- Select the towel and press enter over the heart.
- press enter where both the pink arrows are pointing over the lungs.
- Select the cleaver and press enter rapidly while moving the cursor over the dashed line.
- Head out to the main room and up the stairs.
- Head up the stairs, back to the room the bunnies were cleaning.
- Light all the candles. If there are issues, exit the room and enter it again.
- Go to the chest in the closest right corner of the room and interact with it for the Hiding Hole Key.
- Go up the stairs for an interaction where Bread will sell out the mushroom bunny.
Go up the stairs again and down the trap door. There, you can see a rabbit slumped over. This will initiate a cut scene.
- Go up the ladders and to the roof for another cut scene.
- Interact with the trap door in the corner to go back into the castle.
- Head into the main room of the castle and stop by the little pink bunny on the stairs to give them the paintings.
- Head outside.
- Talk to the big pink bunny with the amputated ear and big eyebrows.
- Go left to go into the forest.
- Continue left until you reach a little round clearing and a cutscene is triggered.
